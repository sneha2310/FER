Face Emotion Recognition

In future, face image recognition plays a vital role in multi-cultural visual communication systems, for emotion translation between cultures which may be considered analogous to speech recognition. Detection of vocal expressions of emotions is also done by it. We found that human beings recognize anger, happiness, sadness by their visual appearance, compared to voice only detection.As a conclusion, we propose a method of facial emotion detection by using hybrid approach, which uses facial emotion recognition.
